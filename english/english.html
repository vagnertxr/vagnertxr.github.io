WHO AM I?

Geographer, graduated from the University of Brasília (UnB), with a Master’s degree in Applied Geosciences and Geodynamics from the same institution.

I am currently a Cartography and Geoprocessing Technician at IBAMA, working as an externally contracted professional. My role involves environmental monitoring, map creation, and satellite image processing, contributing to environmental analyses and data-driven decision-making processes.

I have also worked as a research support scholar at the University of Brasília, focusing on the collection, processing, and dissemination of geographic data on WebGIS platforms (ArcGIS Online). My responsibilities included developing dashboards for spatial data visualization and analysis, as well as implementing and managing spatial database systems using tools such as QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, and PostgreSQL.

I have knowledge in SQL (PostgreSQL + PostGIS), Python and libraries applied to Data Science and Geoprocessing, as well as in database modeling and implementation.

I have over 5 years of experience with Geographic Information Systems and have been working with database systems for 1 year. Recently, I started my journey in front-end development with the aim of improving my skills in WebGIS.
