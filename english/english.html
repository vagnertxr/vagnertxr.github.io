About

Who I am

Geographer (BSc) and Master (MSc) in Applied Geosciences and Geodynamics from the University of Brasília (UnB), with a career focused on geoprocessing, cartography, and geospatial data analysis.

Currently, I provide technical services in geoprocessing and cartography for IBAMA (as an outsourced collaborator), developing geospatial solutions to support field operations, as well as products within the PAMGIA platform. I also implemented Rizoma — an automated route map generator built in R and Python — as a new feature of the Crotalus system (automated prioritization of deforestation alerts).

Previously, I served as a technical research support fellow at UnB, focusing on data structuring for WebGIS platforms (ArcGIS Online) and dashboard creation. My master's research was directed towards studies on mobility structures and urban networks.
