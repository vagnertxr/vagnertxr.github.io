WHO AM I?

Geographer graduated from the University of Brasília (UnB), Master's student in the Graduate Program in Applied Geosciences and Geodynamics also at UnB.

Currently, I am a research support fellow at the University of Brasília, working on the collection, processing, and dissemination of geographic data on the WebGIS platform (ArcGIS Online) and creating dashboards for the visualization and analysis of spatial data. I also work on the implementation and management of spatial database systems, using tools such as QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, and PostgreSQL.

I have knowledge in SQL (PostgreSQL + PostGIS), Python and libraries applied to Data Science and Geoprocessing, as well as in database modeling and implementation.

I have over 5 years of experience with Geographic Information Systems and have been working with database systems for 1 year. Recently, I started my journey in front-end development with the aim of improving my skills in WebGIS.
